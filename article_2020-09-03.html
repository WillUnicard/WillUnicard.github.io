Unicard Appoints Sean Dickinson as CEO to Drive Business Growth

Company builds on its Mobility-as-a-Service and Account-Based Ticketing offerings

Wimborne, UK. September 3rd, Unicard LTD, a provider of smart ticketing and payment solutions to the public transport sector, today announced that it has appointed Sean Dickinson to the post of Chief Executive Officer (CEO). Dickinson, who previously held senior roles at Ericsson and Synchronoss Technologies, will oversee the expansion of the business as Unicard establishes its position in new markets.

Smart ticketing is becoming the standard model for all modes of public and commercial transport throughout the UK. Local Authorities, bus and rail operators are all adopting innovative smart ticketing solutions to underpin travel schemes. Integrated travel schemes that incorporate multiple modes of transport including car, bus, and cycle hire as well as electric scooter rentals are becoming far more commonplace.

Unicard is well placed to help customers take advantage of new trends and initiatives in transport. Its Account-Based Ticketing (ABT) and Mobility-as-a-Service (MaaS) product lines support these new dynamics, enabling Local Authorities and other travel operators to deliver integrated smart ticketing that operates across multiple modes of transport.

Sean Dickinson, CEO of Unicard, said: “The transport landscape is changing. This is driven in part by the current global situation, but also because commuters and passengers demand greater flexibility. This has prompted both Local Authorities and commercial operators to explore greener travel initiatives and to look at ways to supplement public transport with new vehicles and bikes”.

He added: “Smart ticketing technologies are helping Local Authorities and other transport providers to meet new requirements, expand travel schemes and improve the customer experience. This has opened up new market opportunities for Unicard”.

“Unicard is a well-respected brand, renowned for the quality of its products and delivery of its services, along with its willingness to collaborate with customers and partners. I intend to build on these values as we enter the next phase in the company’s development”, said Sean.

Peter Verept, Chairman of Unicard, explained: “Unicard has a strong heritage in the smart ticketing space. We have long-standing relationships with customers because of our commitment to delivering an excellent service”.

“We have evolved to meet the needs of the market. Subsequently, our ABT and MaaS products are helping to reshape the environment as our customers demand more intelligent and flexible travel solutions”, Peter added.

He concluded: “We decided to strengthen the management team and I’m delighted to be introducing Sean to the company during this exciting period of growth and transition”.

Unicard’s fully hosted and managed smart ticketing and payment solutions have been deployed by Local Authorities across the UK to support ENCTS / concessionary travel, and a wide range of public transport schemes. The company operates across multiple transport segments including e-scooters, bus and rail, working with providers to deliver smart ticketing programmes. Its ABT and MaaS product lines spearhead a diverse portfolio that offers customers a comprehensive suite of managed solutions.

Sean Dickinson - CEO Unicard Ltd
