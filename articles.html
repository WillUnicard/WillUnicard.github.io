Unicard Enables Northamptonshire ENCTS

Online service enables elderly and disabled bus passengers to obtain concessionary travel cards.

Unicard is pleased to announce that its fully hosted and managed smart ticketing solution has been implemented by Northamptonshire County Council to provide an online service that allows elderly and disabled residents to apply for, renew and replace concessionary travel cards.

Unicard Delivers ITSO on Mobile

Unicard are the engine behind ITSO on mobile, enabling ITSO tickets on Android.

In 2018, ITSO Ltd, along with Idemia and Unicard delivered the ability to use the ITSO national smartcard on mobile devices. As smartphones become our wallet, ITSO on Mobile promises to make ticketing better and smarter for the travelling public.

The solution was launched working together with Google and the implementation makes the ITSO card emulation available on Google Pay. For further information, please check ITSO

Unicard Smart Ticketing Powers TFWM

Unicard systems power West Midlands public transport operations. In 2015, Euclid Ltd in partnership with Unicard won the Transport for the West Midlands framework tender for the provision of:

  • HOPS/AMS (Unicard)
  • CMS (Unicard)
  • Bureau card production (Euclid)
  • Reimbursement/settlement (Euclid)
  • Customer Self Service Portals concession and commerical (Unicard)

The existing HOPS/AMS was successfully migrated and the scheme has subsequently introduced a multitude of new products and applications. Since this initial project, Unicard has gone on to develop and deliver the Swift on Mobile App, featuring the new ITSO emulation on Google Pay. For further information, please check Network West Midlands and ITSO
